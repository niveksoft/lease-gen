# PET ADDENDUM TO TENANCY AGREEMENT

This Pet Addendum is attached to and forms part of the Residential Tenancy Agreement between:

**Landlord:** ${LANDLORD_NAMES}\
**Tenant:** ${TENANT_NAMES}\
**Rental Address:** ${PROPERTY_ADDRESS}

## 1. Permitted Pet(s)

Tenant is allowed to keep the following pet(s) at the rental unit:\

- Type: ${PET_TYPE}
- Name: ${PET_NAME}
- Description: ${PET_BREED_DESCRIPTION}

No other pets are permitted without the Landlord's written consent.

## 2. Monthly Pet Fee and Rent

Tenant agrees to pay a monthly pet fee of $40. This fee is included as part of the total monthly rent of $2,190, which includes:

- Rent: $2,150
- Pet Fee: $40

The pet fee is non-refundable and payable with rent on the usual rent due date.

## 3. Tenant Responsibilities

- Tenant agrees to keep the pet under control and prevent property damage or disturbance to neighbors.
- Tenant is responsible for cleaning up after the pet inside and outside the property.
- Tenant must comply with City of Calgary pet licensing and bylaws, including obtaining all required pet licenses.
- Tenant must ensure pet vaccinations are current and provide proof if requested.
- Tenant is liable for any damage caused by the pet beyond normal wear and tear.
- Tenant shall maintain valid renters insurance covering pet liability.

## 4. Nuisance and Removal

- If the pet causes a nuisance or damage, the Landlord may require the tenant to remove the pet with 10 days' written notice.
- Failure to remove the pet may lead to tenancy termination.

## 5. General

All other terms of the original tenancy agreement remain in effect. Changes to this addendum require written consent of both parties.
